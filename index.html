Algorithms: Forecasting & Correlation

📈 Linear Regression (OLS)
slope (m) = (n·Σxy − Σx·Σy)
÷ (n·Σx² − (Σx)²)

intercept (b) = (Σy − m·Σx) / n

predict(x) = m·x + b

Application

Time variable x = array index. Generates 5 future forecasts. Slope sign interpreted as upward/downward trend.

🌡️ Pearson Correlation
r(A,B) = Σ[(Aᵢ−Ā)(Bᵢ−B̄)]
÷ √[Σ(Aᵢ−Ā)²·Σ(Bᵢ−B̄)²]

Range: −1 to +1

Color Encoding

Dark green (r>0.7) strong positive | Purple (diagonal=1.0) | Dark red (r<−0.7) strong negative

Performance

Limited to 6 numeric columns. O(36×n) complexity. Computed once on file load, cached in state.
